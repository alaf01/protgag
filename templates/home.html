In our group, we apply computational tools to analyze properties of glycosaminoglycans (GAGs), negatively charged sulfated periodic polysaccharides, and develop new approaches to study the GAG-containing biomolecular systems more effectively. Our work is useful for theoretical rationale in the development of novel strategies for tissue regeneration, where GAGs play a crucial role.
